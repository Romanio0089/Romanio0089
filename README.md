### Who am I

Hi, I'm Romanio0089, an IT professional, gamer and retro computer enthusiast, having around 100 computers in my collection.

I like working on projects related to old tech, and I can program more or less in C#, HTML, CSS, Java, Python, and Batch. I like developing games in Unity, however all of my games are currently private (because they are a huge mess), but one will be released soon from GameBox Studio.

### Projects
 
* **Vir0z4 Network**: a holdings group of some sort, it assembles a couple of IT and gaming related projects ([vir0z4.com](https://vir0z4.com))
* **OpenFreebox Revived**: a reverse engineering project for the Freebox Revolution V6 ([re-openfreebox.github.io](https://re-openfreebox.github.io))

I also work on subsidiaries of Vir0z4 Network:
* **The VAIO Library**: the (better) Wikipedia for all Sony VAIO laptop computers ([vaiolibrary.com](https://vaiolibrary.com))
* **GameBox Studio**: game development studio, based on a Discord community ([gamebox-studio.com](https://gamebox-studio.com))
* **VeryPVP**: Minecraft Java server, featuring: Kit-PVP, a new concept called Rush-PVP, parkour, and more... (currently on hiatus)

I post some of my stuff on my personal website [downtherack.free.fr](http://downtherack.free.fr). My other socials can be found on there.
